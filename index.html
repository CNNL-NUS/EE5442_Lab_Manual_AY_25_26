EE5442 Memory Technologies AY-25/26

This course is offered from the Department of Electrical Engineering (EE) under College of Design and Engineering (CDE) at the National University of Singapore (NUS) for the graduate students during the spring semester. It focuses on the different aspects of memory technologies and their applications in the growing AI industry. This lab is tailored to gain hands on experience on developing designs and systems that pertain to memory devices and neural networks.

The lectures are bifurcated into three segments which will be taught by three Professors from the Department of Electrical Engineering (EE) .

The GAs deliver the lab sessions and clarify doubts of the students in their tasks/assignments for this course. Students can contact them thorough email for any support regarding the resources, labs and projects. The contact of GAs attached to the course for this semester can be found in the page below.

The complete course material and other accesses are also available on Canvas.
